###### COLOR PALETTE
# Brand & UX Colors

Applications inside Drive ecosystem must match a visual identity, so we include a palette of 19 
minimalistic colors (+ themed variables) that can be used to style your applications.

This palette can be extended and used as you want but we recommend to keep in mind Drive design approach, less is more.

> **Black&White** is our identity!
>
> Keep everything **minimalistic** and **not too colorful**
